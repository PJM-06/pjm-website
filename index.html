About Us

PJM Electrical Design and Engineering Services is a professional consultancy firm specializing in comprehensive electrical and electronics design, simulation, drafting, and compliance documentation. We serve clients across the Philippines in residential, commercial, mid-rise, and industrial sectors.

  • Electrical load computations, fault current analysis, and voltage drop evaluation
  • Power distribution system design, panelboard scheduling, and riser diagrams
  • PEC-compliant planning with full permit assistance and licensed engineer sign and seal
  • Photometric lighting simulations using Dialux EVO for accurate lux level planning
  • Preparation of detailed schematic diagrams, single-line diagrams (SLDs), and construction-ready CAD layouts
  • Precise Bill of Quantities (BOQ) and Bill of Materials (BOM) for project cost estimation
  • Technical prototyping and electronics fabrication assistance for academic or student engineering projects
