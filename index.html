About Us

PJM Electrical Design and Engineering Services is a dynamic, multi-disciplinary engineering firm based in the Philippines that focuses on delivering high-quality, code-compliant, and technically sound electrical design solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial developments.

Our core expertise lies in conducting detailed load computations, voltage drop analysis, short circuit and fault current studies, as well as lighting simulations using advanced software like Dialux and Relux. We produce fully coordinated construction drawings, including CAD plans, panelboard schedules, single-line diagrams, and auxiliary layouts compliant with the Philippine Electrical Code (PEC).

PJM also provides essential design analysis reports, detailed bill of quantities and material estimations (BOQ/BOM), and handles permit acquisition support including engineering sign and seal services. Our firm also supports as-built plan generation and site-based validation for real-world integration.

Mission

To deliver precise, standards-compliant electrical designs that empower infrastructure, ensure safety, and foster technical excellence across the country.

Vision

To become a premier provider of electrical engineering and design services in the Philippines, recognized for innovation, professionalism, and impact.
