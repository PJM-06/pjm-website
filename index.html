About Us

PJM Electrical Design and Engineering Services is a professional firm based in the Philippines that specializes in comprehensive electrical system planning, design, documentation, and permit processing across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

  • Load computations, fault current studies, and voltage drop analysis
  • Lighting simulations using Dialux/Relux and lux level evaluation
  • Distribution layouts, panelboard scheduling, riser diagrams
  • Complete CAD drafting of electrical and auxiliary plans
  • PEC-compliant drawings for permit with sign and seal support
  • As-built plans with technical review and field verification

Mission

To deliver precise, standards-compliant electrical designs that empower infrastructure, ensure safety, and foster technical excellence across the country.

Vision

To become a premier provider of electrical engineering and design services in the Philippines, recognized for innovation, professionalism, and impact.
